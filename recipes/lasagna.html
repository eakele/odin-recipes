lasagna

Here is a lasagna recipe

The main ingredients are:

  • Pasta
  • Tomato
  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Oil or butter

Steps

  1. First coock the onion by adding oil
  2. add tomato and cook for about 3 minutes
  3. add the meat and cook it for about 15 minutes
  4. cook the pasta
  5. Add the sauce on the drained pasta
  6. cook in the oven for about 20 minutes

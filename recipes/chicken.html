Chicken Vegie Roast

Here is a chicken vegie roast recipe

The main ingredients are:

  • Chicken
  • Onion and some vegies
  • Oil or butter
  • Spices

Steps

  1. First marinate the chicken with oil and spices
  2. add marinated chicken and the vegies into oven safe container
  3. Cook in the oven for 45 minute high heat
